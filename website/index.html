Welcome

Welcome, and thanks for stopping by. I'm a Strategic Advisor at Workato, an enterprise orchestration and agentic-AI platform, and I live in Barcelona with my family and our dog Sahi. The work I do at Workato sits in two halves. The first is hands-on: I lead the strategic engagements with the company's largest enterprise customers — designing the governance, the lifecycle architecture, and the agentic-AI patterns they need to grow their Workato practice across their organizations. The second is institutional: I'm the author of GEARS, the customer governance and maturity framework Workato uses to take enterprises from initial implementation through governed, scaled adoption, and a core contributor to its agentic / Model Context Protocol extension.

I came into enterprise software through engineering — Node.js, GraphQL, integration architecture — and built the post-sale technical-services function at Thought Industries from inside that role over six years, taking it from a one-engineer escalation surface to a tiered Solutions and Platform Engineering organization.

Before tech, I spent sixteen years in higher education: a Brown PhD in modern culture and media, then professor of film and digital media at Brown and peer institutions, then senior policy and communications strategist to the president of Rhode Island School of Design. The through-line is a structural instinct — how systems are put together, how they move, and how the patterns inside one domain show up in another.

If you're here because you're hiring, I'm open to senior advisor, principal solutions architect, and senior pre-sales roles based in Europe, or remote from Europe to the US. The resume below has the long version. If you're here for another reason — a friend, a former student, a colleague, a curious stranger — I'm glad you came by.
